Software Requirements Specification (SRS)
BARAKA SaaS Platform
1. Introduction
1.1 Purpose

This document describes the requirements and architecture for a small-scale village delivery SaaS platform that connects:

Customers
Local shops/restaurants
Delivery drivers (“طيارين”)

The platform allows customers to order products from nearby shops using a web application, while drivers handle deliveries and shop owners manage orders and products.

1.2 Project Goal

Build a lightweight and scalable delivery platform for villages and small towns where digital ordering systems are limited or unavailable.

1.3 Scope

The system will provide:

Online ordering
Product browsing
Cart & checkout
Delivery assignment
Order tracking
Shop management dashboard
Driver management system
Admin dashboard

Initial version will be web-based only.

14. Conclusion

The platform aims to digitize local village commerce by providing a lightweight delivery ecosystem connecting shops, customers, and delivery drivers in a scalable and cost-effective way.

The MVP focuses on simplicity, fast deployment, and real-world usability while maintaining scalability for future SaaS expansion.
